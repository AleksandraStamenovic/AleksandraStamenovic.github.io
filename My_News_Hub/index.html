Grand Opening of “Zum Griechen”

Mark your calendars for Sunday, 26. May 2024, as a new dining and partying experience is set to arrive to the neighbourhood.

“Zum Griechen”, the new culinary experience of the neighbourhood proudly introduced by the new CEO, Franz Kafka. The grand opening takes place at Naturfreundestrasse 35, Waakirchen, starting at 18 o’clock.

Guests can look forward to a delicious Greek cuisine, as well as a band that will feature traditional Greek music, which will transport us all to sandy Greek beaches.
And for our nightbird Guests, there will be a DJ that will preform into the late hours (specifically 3 hours after midnight).
However, that is not everything that this new opening has to offer. For all ladies visiting “Zum Griechen”, there is a special treat: Complementary drinks courtesy of the house.

We can't wait to welcome guests to 'Zum Griechen,' where an authentic Greek dining experience awaits. Our goal is to transport our patrons to the heart of Greece through our flavors, music, and warm hospitality.
- Franz Kafka, our welcoming host.

Make sure not to miss out in this special night, where we will together celebrate Greek cuisine, music and hospitality. Join us at “Zum Griechen“ for delicious food, good music and wonderful company.
