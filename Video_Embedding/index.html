How to embed a video

Step 1

Find a video on You Tube that you want to add to your website.

Step 2

When you have found your video, click share. In the popup window click embed.

Step 3

At the right side of the popup window, you will see the line of code that you should copy.

Step 4

Find a place in your code where you want the video to appear and paste.

This is how it will look at the end.
